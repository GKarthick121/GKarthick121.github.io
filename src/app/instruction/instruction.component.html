1.The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
2.Player 1 is X, next player is O.
3.Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
4.The first player to get 3 of him/her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
5.When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
